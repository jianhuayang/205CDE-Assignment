Intro

Founded in 2010 by Sarah, Tom and Amber, Phoenix Coffee aspired to bring great coffee to Coventry, one cup at a time. With chain coffee shops popping up on every corner, we wanted to give local people to experience amazing coffee from an independent coffee shop.

We source all out coffee from UK rotaries, who work with farmers across the across the world to deliver exclusive small batch roasts, meaning once it is gone it is gone!
